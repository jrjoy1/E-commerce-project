Orders & Shipping

We offer three shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over $500)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($19.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($39.99)

Shipping times may vary during peak seasons or due to weather conditions.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can:

  • Use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Log into your account and view order history
  • Contact our customer service team for assistance

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We're working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations. Please check back for updates!

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, orders are processed for shipping and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately at (555) 123-4567 if you need to make changes.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be:

  • In original condition and packaging
  • Unopened (for software and consumables)
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Include all accessories and documentation

Custom-configured items may have different return terms.

To return an item:

  1. Log into your account and go to order history
  2. Select the order and click "Return Item"
  3. Choose your reason for return and print the return label
  4. Package the item securely and attach the return label
  5. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank or credit card company.

Yes! We offer exchanges for the same product in a different size, color, or model (if price difference applies). Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.
